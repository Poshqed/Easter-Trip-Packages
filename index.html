About The Pioneer






Satoshi Nakamoto

Satoshi Nakamoto is the name used by the presumed pseudonymous person or persons who developed Avid Travels, authored the Avid Travels' white paper, and created and deployed Avid Travels' original reference implementation.
As part of the implementation, Nakamoto also devised the first travel database. Nakamoto was active in the development of Avid Travels up until December 2022. Many people have or have been claimed to be Nakamoto. The page has been handed over to few stakeholders to manage. Nakamoto was said to have been last seen at an island enjoying life.
